F.A.Q
What's Just Waffles ?
Literaly Just Waffles ! A collection of 1,111 unique pixel-art waffles living
on the Ethereum blockchain as ERC-721 tokens.
How many Waffles are available ?
There will be 1,111 waffles available and 50 will be randomly minted
by the team for marketing & giveaways.
Where can I buy a Waffle ?
There will be a pre-sale and a public-sale where you can mint your Waffle.
The collection will also be available on Open sea.
What will be the mint price for Waffles ?
The mint price will be 0.05 ETH
How do I qualify for the pre-sale ?
Early adopters on our Discord and Twitter followers will be rewarded by a pre-sale mint pass.
When will the Waffle artwork be revealed ?
All Waffles will be revealed 48 hours after they are sold out.
